# WriteAway - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

## What We Collect

### Free Version:
- **Nothing.** Your writing stays on your device.
- We don't collect personal information, analytics, or usage data.

### Premium Version:
- Email (for license delivery only)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe/Gumroad - we never see card details)
- License activation data (to prevent sharing)

## How We Use Data

- **Email:** Send license codes and important updates only
- **License data:** Verify premium access
- We NEVER:
  - Sell your data
  - Track your writing
  - Share with third parties
  - Use analytics/cookies (beyond essential functionality)

## Your Writing

- **Stored locally** on your device only
- **Cloud sync** (Premium, optional): Encrypted end-to-end
- We cannot access your writing content
- You can export and delete anytime

## Cookies & Tracking

- **No tracking cookies**
- **No analytics** (Google Analytics, etc.)
- **LocalStorage only:** Save your preferences and premium status
- **No third-party scripts** except payment processing

## Your Rights

You can:
- Export all your data (download .txt files)
- Delete your data (clear browser storage)
- Request account deletion
- Opt out of emails (unsubscribe link)

## Children's Privacy

WriteAway is not intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

## Security

- HTTPS encryption
- No passwords stored (license code system)
- Premium verification uses secure tokens

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We'll notify users of any changes via the app.

Data stored locally on your device only. Cloud sync is not currently available.

**Summary:** We respect your privacy. Your writing is yours. We collect minimal data only for essential functions.
